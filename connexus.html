Overview
Urban densification is redefining how we live. Connexus is a modular furniture system born for hybrid spaces — where you work, rest, and socialize in the same room. It doesn't just organize. It transforms.
01
The Problem

As cities densify and living spaces shrink, the furniture we own can no longer afford to be static. A desk is a desk. A shelf is a shelf. But what happens when your apartment is also your office, your studio, and your living room — all at once?

Connexus was designed to answer that question. The brief called for a system that could adapt intuitively through simple reconfigurations — inviting movement and exploration rather than locking users into a single layout.

The Process

Developed the full visual identity for Connexus — a clean, architectural system that mirrors the logic of the furniture itself. Every asset was built on the same modular thinking: simple units that combine into something greater.

Designed a comprehensive process book documenting the journey from brief to final form, an assembly guide mapping every possible modular configuration, and the overall visual language that ties the system together across all touchpoints.

Assembly Guide

A modular system is only as powerful as its instructions. The Connexus assembly guide maps every possible configuration — from the simplest stack to the most complex spatial arrangement — so users can explore freely and build with confidence.
